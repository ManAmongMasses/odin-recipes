Pesto pasta

Ingredients:

  • Pack of pasta (your choice)
  • Red/green pesto (your choice)
  • 1 onion
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Cheese (grated or block, your choice)

Steps:

  1. Preheat water in a pot until boiling.
  2. Boil pasta al dente (should take 10 minutes or so, but test individual pieces before removing from heat).
  3. SLice the onion. Cook in olive oil until translucent, then stir with pesto and seasoning (salt/pepper to taste).
  4. Add the pesto mixture to the above. Add cheese to your preference.
