Cheesecake

Ingredients:

  • 1 pack digestive biscuits
  • 1/4 cup salted butter, melted
  • 1/2 cup white sugar, divided
  • 5 ounces dark chocolate
  • 2 tablespoons unsweetened cocoa powder, sifted
  • 2 or 3 tablespoonshot water, or otherwise as needed
  • 1 tub cream cheese, softened
  • 1/2 cup double cream

Steps:

  1. Crush biscuits with a rolling pin, pestle and mortar, or whatever tool you fancy, until ground into small bits.
  2. Mix with melted butter and sugar, then press combined mixture into the bottom of a tray/pan, dependent on intended shape of cheesecake.
  3. Refrigerate while we focus on the next steps.
  4. Melt chocolate in 10-15 second bursts in microwave, to avoid burning. Ensure you stir between bursts.
  5. WHisk together cocoa powder and water, enough to form a paste.
  6. Beat the cream cheese and the remaining sugar into the cocoa mixture until combined.
  7. Beat the double cream in a glass bowl until soft peaks form (until you've got yourself proper whipped cream). Fold the cooled chocolate in.
  8. Fold the two mixtures together (chocolate cream and cream cheeese). Spread the resulting mixture on top of the refrigerated crust.
  9. Freeze/refrigerate until firm enough to hold shape. It's now ready for eating!
