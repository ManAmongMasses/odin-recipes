Lasagne, mum's way

A recipe that has stood the test of time, both through the centuries, and through childhood/adolescence to now!

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 green bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • Salt and peppper to taste
  • 2 (6 pound) cans tomato paste
  • 2 ¼ cups water
  • pint part-skim ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 8 lasagne noodles, cooked and drained
